<p><p>The stretch socks market can be categorized primarily into gradient socks and anti-embolism socks. Gradient socks, designed to provide varying levels of compression from the ankle to the calf, improve blood circulation and reduce swelling, making them popular for athletes and individuals with circulatory issues. In contrast, anti-embolism socks offer consistent compression, specifically aimed at preventing deep vein thrombosis (DVT) during extended periods of immobility, such as during hospitalization or long flights. Both serve important health and wellness needs.</p></p>
