<p><p>RTD (Resistance Temperature Detector) sensors are precision temperature sensors that utilize the principle that the electrical resistance of a material changes with temperature. The market features two primary types: wire-wound RTD sensors, which consist of a fine wire coiled around a core to provide high accuracy and stability, and thin film RTD sensors, which use a thin film of platinum deposited on a substrate for a compact design and fast response time. Both types are widely used in industrial and laboratory applications.</p></p>
