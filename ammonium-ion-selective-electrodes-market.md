<p><ul><li>Liquid Membrane</li><li>PVC Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ammonium ion selective electrodes (ISEs) are crucial in monitoring ammonium ion concentrations in various applications. The market is primarily divided into two types: liquid membrane and PVC membrane electrodes. Liquid membrane electrodes utilize a solvent-based membrane that selectively allows ammonium ions to pass, offering high sensitivity. In contrast, PVC membrane electrodes use a polyvinyl chloride matrix to trap ionophores, providing a stable and cost-effective solution. Both types play essential roles in environmental monitoring, agriculture, and industrial processes.</p></p>
