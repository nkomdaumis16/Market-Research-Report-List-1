<p><p>The polyester-based polyurethane prepolymer market is categorized into high viscosity and low viscosity segments. High viscosity prepolymers are characterized by thicker consistency, offering enhanced durability and resistance, making them suitable for applications in coatings and adhesives that require robust performance. In contrast, low viscosity prepolymers flow more easily, allowing for better penetration and wetting in applications like foams and elastomers, where flexibility and ease of processing are essential. Each type serves distinct industrial needs based on performance and application requirements.</p></p>
