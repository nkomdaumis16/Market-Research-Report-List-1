<p><p>Dissolved Gas Analyzers (DGA) are essential for monitoring the condition of insulating oils in transformers by analyzing gases dissolved within them, indicating potential faults. Market types include Vacuum Extraction or Rack Method, which efficiently extracts gases by creating a vacuum. The Head Space Extraction Method utilizes the gas above the liquid's surface for analysis. The Stripper Column Method employs a column to strip gases from the liquid, providing reliable results for early fault detection and equipment maintenance.</p></p>
