<p><p>The nickel brazing filler metals market encompasses various types, including powders, brazing pastes, tapes, foils, and preforms. Powders are utilized for precision applications, providing a uniform layer for joining. Brazing pastes offer ease of application and control, ideal for intricate designs. Tapes provide flexibility and can be easily applied to surfaces, while foils are commonly used for even heat distribution in larger assemblies. Preforms are standardized shapes, simplifying the brazing process by ensuring consistent joint quality and efficiency.</p></p>
