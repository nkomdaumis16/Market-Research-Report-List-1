<p><strong>The Apheresis Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Donor Apheresis</li><li>Therapeutic Apheresis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Apheresis machines are categorized into two main types: donor and therapeutic apheresis. Donor apheresis involves the collection of specific blood components, such as platelets or plasma, from healthy individuals for transfusion or research purposes. Therapeutic apheresis, on the other hand, is a medical procedure that removes harmful substances from a patient's blood, such as excess lipids or autoantibodies, to treat various medical conditions. Both types play crucial roles in modern medicine, enhancing patient care and supporting blood donation efforts.</p></p>
