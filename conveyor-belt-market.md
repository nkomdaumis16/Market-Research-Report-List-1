<p><p>The conveyor belt market comprises several key types, including multiply fabric conveyor belts, steel cord conveyor belts, and solid woven conveyor belts. Multiply fabric conveyor belts offer flexibility and versatility, commonly used in light to medium-duty applications. Steel cord conveyor belts are designed for heavy-duty usage, providing high tensile strength and durability, ideal for long-distance transport. Solid woven conveyor belts feature a robust design that resists abrasion and impacts, making them suitable for challenging environments such as mining and bulk material handling.</p></p>
