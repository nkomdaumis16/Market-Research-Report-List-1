<p><p>BGA rework stations are essential tools for repairing and replacing Ball Grid Array (BGA) components on PCBs. There are two predominant market types: Hot Air and Infrared (IR). Hot Air stations use a stream of heated air to soften solder, allowing for component removal and placement. In contrast, Infrared rework stations utilize directed infrared radiation to achieve precise heating, ensuring uniform temperature distribution. Both types cater to different user preferences and application needs in electronics manufacturing and repair.</p></p>
