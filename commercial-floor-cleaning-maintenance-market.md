<p><ul><li>Corrective Maintenance</li><li>Preventive Maintenance</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial floor cleaning maintenance market encompasses two primary types: corrective and preventive maintenance. Corrective maintenance involves addressing issues after they arise, such as repairing damage or deep cleaning to restore floor aesthetics and functionality. In contrast, preventive maintenance focuses on routine cleaning and inspections to prevent problems before they occur, extending floor life and maintaining cleanliness. Both approaches are essential for ensuring a safe, appealing environment in commercial spaces, enhancing operational efficiency and longevity of flooring materials.</p></p>
