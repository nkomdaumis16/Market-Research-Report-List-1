<p><p>The Interventional X-ray System market is primarily divided into two types: Single Plane Interventional X-ray Systems and Bi-plane Interventional X-ray Systems. Single Plane systems utilize a single fluoroscopy source and detector to provide real-time imaging for procedures, making them suitable for a range of interventions. Bi-plane systems, on the other hand, incorporate two planes of imaging to offer enhanced visualization from multiple angles, which is particularly beneficial for complex procedures requiring precision and detailed imaging. Both types cater to various medical specialties, enhancing diagnostic and therapeutic capabilities.</p></p>
