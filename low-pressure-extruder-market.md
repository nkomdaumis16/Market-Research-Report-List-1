<p><strong>The Low Pressure Extruder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-screw Extruder</li><li>Twin-screw Extruder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The low pressure extruder market includes two primary types: single-screw and twin-screw extruders. Single-screw extruders utilize a single helical screw to process materials, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness, commonly used in applications like plastics and food processing. In contrast, twin-screw extruders feature two intermeshing screws that enhance mixing, flexibility, and better handling of varying materials, making them ideal for complex formulations in industries such as pharmaceuticals and advanced plastics. Both types cater to specific processing needs and applications.</p></p>
