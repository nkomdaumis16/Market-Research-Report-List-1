Coal-based activated carbon is known for its high adsorption capacity and durability, making it suitable for industrial applications. Wood-based activated carbon is prized for its high pore volume and surface area, often used in air and water purification. Coconut-based activated carbon is recognized for its sustainability and is commonly employed in applications requiring finely powdered or granular forms, such as in food and beverage processing.</p></p>
