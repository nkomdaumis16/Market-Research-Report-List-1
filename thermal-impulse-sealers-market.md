<p><p>The thermal impulse sealers market consists of two main types: handheld and fixed sealers. Handheld sealers are portable, allowing for easy operation in various locations, making them ideal for small-scale or occasional sealing tasks. In contrast, fixed sealers are designed for continuous, high-volume sealing applications, optimizing production efficiency in industrial settings. Both types utilize heat to create airtight seals, but they cater to different operational needs, emphasizing flexibility versus productivity in packaging solutions.</p></p>
