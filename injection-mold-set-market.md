<p><p>The injection mold set market is primarily divided into aluminum molds and steel molds. Aluminum molds are lightweight, cost-effective, and suitable for low to medium production runs, providing quick turnaround times. They are ideal for prototyping and shorter project lifecycles. In contrast, steel molds are more durable and can withstand higher production volumes, making them suitable for larger-scale manufacturing.
