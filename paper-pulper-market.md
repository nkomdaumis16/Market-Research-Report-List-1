<p><strong>The Paper Pulper Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hydrapilper</li><li>Drum Hydrapulper</li><li>Broke Pulper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The paper pulper market consists of various types, including hydrapulpers, drum hydrapulpers, and broke pulpers. Hydrapulpers use high-speed paddles in a vat to effectively disintegrate paper waste into pulp, suitable for recycling. Drum hydrapulpers operate in a cylindrical drum, offering continuous processing and higher efficiency for large volumes. Broke pulpers specifically handle production waste or "broke," converting it back into usable pulp, minimizing waste and enhancing sustainability in paper production processes. Each type serves distinct operational needs in recycling.</p></p>
