<p><p>The wood preservative coatings market comprises three main types: water-based, solvent-based, and others. Water-based coatings utilize water as a solvent, offering low toxicity and easy cleanup, making them environmentally friendly. Solvent-based coatings, on the other hand, contain organic solvents, providing durable protection but often emit higher levels of VOCs. The others category includes specialty formulations such as oil-based and hybrid options, catering to specific applications and performance requirements, ensuring effective wood preservation across diverse environments.</p></p>
