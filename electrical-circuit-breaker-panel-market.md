<p><ul><li>"Ampere Breaker"</li><li>"Safety Breaker"</li><li>"Ground-Fault Circuit Breaker"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrical circuit breaker panel market is segmented into three main types: Ampere Breakers, Safety Breakers, and Ground-Fault Circuit Breakers (GFCI). Ampere Breakers are rated by their current-carrying capacity, offering protection against overloads. Safety Breakers enhance user safety by detecting and interrupting fault currents, while GFCIs specifically protect against ground faults that can cause electrocution by monitoring current imbalances. Each type addresses unique safety and performance needs in residential, commercial, and industrial applications.</p></p>
