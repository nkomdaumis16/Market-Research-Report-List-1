<p><p>The engine intake filter market is primarily divided into two types: wet and dry filters. Wet-type filters utilize a liquid medium, typically oil, to trap contaminants, enhancing filtration efficiency and reusability. In contrast, dry-type filters use specialized materials to capture particles without moisture, offering ease of maintenance and cost-effectiveness. Both types serve crucial roles in protecting engine components from debris, impacting performance and longevity. Their selection depends on factors like application, environmental conditions, and desired filtration efficacy.</p></p>
