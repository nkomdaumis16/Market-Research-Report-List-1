<p><ul><li>Single-mode</li><li>Multi-mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fiber optic collimators are devices used to focus light from a fiber optic source into a parallel beam. The market is divided into two main types: single-mode and multi-mode collimators. Single-mode collimators are designed for long-distance communication, using a single light path for reduced signal loss and higher data rates, making them ideal for telecommunication applications. In contrast, multi-mode collimators support multiple light paths, allowing for shorter distances and higher data throughput in local area networks and data centers.</p></p>
