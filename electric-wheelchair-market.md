<p><strong>The Electric Wheelchair Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry-Battery Electric Wheelchairs</li><li>Wet-Battery Electric Wheelchairs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric wheelchair market is divided into two primary types based on battery technology: dry-battery and wet-battery electric wheelchairs. Dry-battery electric wheelchairs use sealed, maintenance-free batteries that offer good energy efficiency and are lightweight, making them suitable for frequent use and easy transport. In contrast, wet-battery electric wheelchairs, typically using lead-acid batteries, provide higher power output but require regular maintenance and are heavier. Each type caters to different user needs, preferences, and budget constraints.</p></p>
