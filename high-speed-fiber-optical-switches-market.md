<p><strong>The High-Speed Fiber Optical Switches Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-mode Fiber Optical Switches</li><li>Multimode Fiber Optical Switches</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-speed fiber optical switches market comprises two main types: single-mode and multimode fiber optical switches. Single-mode fiber optical switches utilize a single light pathway, enabling long-distance communication with low signal loss, ideal for telecommunications and data centers. In contrast, multimode fiber optical switches support multiple light pathways, making them suitable for shorter distances typically found in local area networks (LANs) and data communication within buildings. Both types facilitate high-speed data transmission and enhance network efficiency.</p></p>
