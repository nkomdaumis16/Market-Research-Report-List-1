<p><p>Heterogeneous Networks (HetNets) combine various types of cellular and wireless technologies to enhance coverage, capacity, and user experience. Macro Base Stations provide extensive coverage for wide areas, while Small Cells offer localized capacity boosts in high-density locations. Distributed Antenna Systems (DAS) improve indoor and outdoor signal quality by using multiple antennas linked to a central source. Wi-Fi Access Points supplement mobile networks by providing robust, high-speed internet, especially in enterprise and public environments, ensuring seamless connectivity across different user demands.</p></p>
