<p><ul><li>Internal Releasing Agent</li><li>External Release Agent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The release agents market is categorized into two primary types: internal and external release agents. Internal release agents are additives incorporated directly into materials, such as plastics or composites, enhancing performance and ease of removal during manufacturing processes. In contrast, external release agents are applied to surfaces to create a barrier between the mold and the product, facilitating easy demolding. Both types play crucial roles in various industries, including automotive, aerospace, and construction, by improving production efficiency and product quality.</p></p>
