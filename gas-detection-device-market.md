<p><p>The gas detection device market is categorized into three main types: detectors, transmitters, and controllers. Detectors are devices that sense the presence of harmful gases, providing immediate alerts. Transmitters convert the detected signals into readable data, enabling remote monitoring. Controllers serve as the central unit that processes data from detectors and transmitters, facilitating system management and response actions. Together, these components enhance safety by efficiently monitoring and responding to hazardous gas levels in various environments.</p></p>
