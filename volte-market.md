<p><strong>The VoLTE Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>VoIMS</li><li>CSFB</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The VoLTE market can be categorized into three main types: VoIMS, CSFB, and others. VoIMS (Voice over IMS) leverages the IP Multimedia Subsystem to provide high-quality voice services over LTE networks. CSFB (Circuit Switched Fallback) allows for voice calls to occur by temporarily switching to 2G or 3G networks when LTE isn’t able to handle the voice call. Other market types may include enhanced services or combinations of these technologies, catering to varied consumer and operator needs.</p></p>
