<p><ul><li>Access Network</li><li>Metropolitan Area Network</li><li>Trunk Network</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical communication network equipment market can be categorized into three main types: Access Networks, Metropolitan Area Networks (MAN), and Trunk Networks. Access Networks connect end-users to the internet, ensuring high-speed broadband access, typically through fiber optics. Metropolitan Area Networks facilitate communication within a city or large campus, linking various networks and providing high-capacity data transfer. Trunk Networks, on the other hand, serve as the backbone of communication systems, connecting multiple networks over long distances, enabling efficient and high-volume data transmission.</p></p>
