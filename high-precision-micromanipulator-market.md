Electric micromanipulators leverage motors and advanced electronics to enable quick and accurate positioning, often featuring programmable functions. Manual micromanipulators, operated by hand, provide direct tactile feedback and simplicity, making them ideal for basic applications. Each type serves distinct operational needs in fields like micro-manufacturing, biotechnology, and materials science.</p></p>
