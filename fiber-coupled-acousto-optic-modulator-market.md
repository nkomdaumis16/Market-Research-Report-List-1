<p><p>The fiber-coupled acousto-optic modulator market is primarily segmented into two types: SM (Single-Mode) Fiber and SMPM (Single-Mode Polarization Maintaining) Fiber. SM Fiber is designed for applications requiring minimal mode dispersion, ensuring high fidelity in optical signals. In contrast, SMPM Fiber maintains polarization while also delivering low-loss performance, making it ideal for sensitive applications like telecommunications and precision measurement. Both types enable efficient coupling of light into modulators, enhancing functionality in various photonics and communication systems.</p></p>
