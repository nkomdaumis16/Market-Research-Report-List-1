<p><p>The gear reduction starter market primarily consists of two types: offset gear reduction starters and planetary gear reduction starters. Offset gear reduction starters utilize an offset design that effectively reduces the motor speed while increasing torque, making them suitable for performance applications. In contrast, planetary gear reduction starters employ a system of gears arranged in a planetary configuration, providing compactness and high efficiency. Both types are essential in enhancing starting power and reliability for various automotive and industrial applications.</p></p>
