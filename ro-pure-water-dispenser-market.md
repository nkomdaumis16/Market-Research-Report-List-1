<p><p>The RO Pure Water Dispenser market is divided into two main types: Point of Use (POU) and Point of Entry (POE) RO Water Dispensers. POU RO Water Dispensers are designed for individual or small-scale use, providing purified water directly at the consumption point, such as kitchens or offices. In contrast, POE RO Water Dispensers serve the entire household or building, purifying water at the entry point before it is distributed through the plumbing system, making it suitable for larger demands.</p></p>
