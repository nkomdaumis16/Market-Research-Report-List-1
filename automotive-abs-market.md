<p><p>The automotive Anti-lock Braking System (ABS) market is categorized into three main types based on channel configuration: One-channel, Three-channel, and Four-channel ABS. One-channel ABS is typically found in older or simpler vehicles, managing a single wheel brake. Three-channel ABS is common in certain light commercial vehicles, allowing independent control of front wheels while combining rear wheel control. Four-channel ABS, prevalent in modern vehicles, offers individual wheel control for enhanced safety and performance, providing optimal braking response under various conditions.</p></p>
